About DMI Engineering College

DMI Engineering College, Aralvaimozhi, Kanyakumari District, is committed to academic excellence, innovation, and student development. Our annual College Day & Sports Fest 2026 celebrates student creativity, technical talent, and sportsmanship. Students from multiple departments participate in cultural programs, technical competitions, and sports tournaments.
